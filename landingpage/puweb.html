CONVOCATION

The President conferred degrees and medals to the successful students. Puducherry Lieutenant Governor Kiran Bedi, Chief Minister V. Narayanasamy, Members of Parliament and Ministers also attend the convocation ceremony. Addressing the gathering, the president lauded University's efforts to implement Swachh Bharat in its campus. He also appreciated university and it's affiliated colleges for adopting 103 villages in and around the campus in order to develop them as model villages.

CULTURAL CUM CONVENTION CENTER

To facilitate conduct of international, national and important University events such as conferrences, workshops, seminars, meetings, placement programmes and cultural events, the University has two big auditoriums, several seminar halls, conference halls, etc. All of them are well-furnished, airconditioned and equipped with modern projection and audio systems with access to internet.

WORLD CLASS LIBRARY

The Library is renowned for its disabled friendly modern infrastructure (80,000 sq. ft)fully air-conditioned, WIFI enabled, services for the Visually Challenged among the proactive services with RFID technology and 24/7 remote access to a collection of 4.4 lakhs, of which print is 2.23 and 2.17 lakhs of e-resources

RESEARCH AND INNOVATION

The University has a strong research culture. About 90% of the faculty have Ph.D. Degrees and are all actively involved in research supervision with independent & collaborative research projects funded by various agencies. More than 650 research scholars are working on the campus pursuing their Ph.D. research and this number is expected to cross 1000 in the next two years.Over two hundred and five research projects are being carried out and this number is progressively increasing. The funding has more than doubled during the last three years. Six departments have FIST-funding from the D.S.T., Ministry of Science & Technology.
